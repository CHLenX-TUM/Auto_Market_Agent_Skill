# Auto_Market_Agent_Skill 🚗🤖

An AI-powered toolkit of agentic skills designed specifically for Product Managers in the global automotive industry. Built to automate manual workflows and accelerate professional growth.

## 🌟 Why I Built This

As a junior product manager working on global automotive products, I quickly realized the learning curve is steep—not just because of the complex market, but because of the sheer volume of manual work. 

The biggest time-sinks in our daily tasks are:
1. **Cross-Regional Spec Comparisons:** As a global PM, comparing our vehicle against dozens of different competitors across various countries is a massive workload. 
2. **Market Charting:** Formatting data and drawing market positioning bubble charts consumes hours of time.
3. **Daily News & Policy Tracking:** Manually monitoring competitor moves and shifting government regulations across global markets is a daily necessity, but incredibly tedious to compile.

That time could be better spent on product strategy, user research, and personal growth. I started building **Auto_Market_Agent_Skill** using Claude Code and other AI agent frameworks to automate these repetitive tasks so junior PMs can focus on high-impact work.

## 🛠️ Core Skills & Features

### 1. 🌍 Global Vehicle Specification Comparison
Dealing with multiple countries means dealing with fragmented competitor landscapes. This agent automates the line-by-line comparison of vehicle specifications. You input your car and target regions, and the agent automatically compiles, aligns, and highlights the advantages and disadvantages against regional competitors, saving hours of manual spreadsheet work.

### 2. 📊 Automated Market Charting (The "Bubble Chart" Agent)
Drawing a bubble chart is a basic but tedious necessity for automotive PMs, often used to map out competitor pricing, sales volumes, and vehicle specifications (like wheelbase). This skill automates data processing and chart generation, instantly turning raw market data into presentation-ready visuals.

### 3. 📝 Auto Show & Trend Report Generator
Staying updated with the fast-paced auto industry is critical. This agent processes information, press releases, and data from major global auto shows to generate comprehensive reports. It easily highlights the latest market shifts and technology trends, ensuring you are always ready for your next presentation.

### 4. 📰 Daily Global Auto Market Scanner
Monitoring the daily pulse of the industry is essential. This agent automatically scans, filters, and summarizes daily news to provide a concise overview of market movements, focusing specifically on:
* **Regional Markets:** Tracking macro and micro trends across the European, Asian, Chinese, and American markets.
* **Key Competitors:** Monitoring strategic moves, pricing changes, and tech releases from major players like Tesla, BYD, and BMW.
* **Local Government Regulations:** Keeping a close eye on critical policy shifts, such as special European tariffs on Chinese OEMs, and European taxation models based on vehicle weight and CO2 emissions.
